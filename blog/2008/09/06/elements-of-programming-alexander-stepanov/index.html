Elements of Programming – Alexander Stepanov

Sep 6th, 2008

Alexander Stepanov (the brains behind the C++ Standard Library) has just posted the latest draft of the first five chapters of his upcoming book “Foundations Of Programming” which is co-authored by Paul McJones. You can view the PDF here.

He currently works at Adobe and has a remit for providing the formalisation of generic programming techniques. This work also provides the basis for the Adobe ASL open-source C++ libraries.

At the ACCU Conference earlier this year I attended the the Adobe ASL talks hosted by Sean Parent, where he said about giving Stepanov the job of formalising the ideas behind generic programming. You can read my write-up of the talk here. I found the talk eye opening and interesting and the ideas behind the approach give them a very solid foundation for reusable components.
