Blogging At 70mph

Fri 15 August 2008 by Garry Bodsworth

I’m in a car travelling at 70mph and I can blog (I’m not driving – honest). Welcome to the future. I’m writing this on a 3G iPhone using the WordPress free app.

It’s nice and simple to use, even offering photoblogging facilities. I like the pared down nature of it because it simply lets you write. It could do with some tweaking in the how to publish or save drafts department though.

All I need now is my jetpack.
